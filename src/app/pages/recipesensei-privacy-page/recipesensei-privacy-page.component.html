RecipeSensei

Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 28, 2026. Last updated: May 1, 2026.

Overview

RecipeSensei respects your privacy. This policy explains what information is used to provide the RecipeSensei mobile app and how you can contact Drake with questions.

Information Used by the App

  • Recipe content you create or import, including titles, ingredients, steps, notes, tags, servings, source links, and optional recipe photos.
  • Feedback or support messages you choose to send.
  • Basic diagnostic and crash information provided by Apple if enabled by your device settings and Apple services.

RecipeSensei does not collect precise location data through the app.

Photos, Camera, and Recipe Import

RecipeSensei may let you select images from your photo library, use the camera for live recipe scanning, or import recipe text and links you choose to share with the app.

Photo and camera access are requested and controlled by iOS system permissions and picker UI. You can revoke access at any time in iOS Settings.

How Information Is Used

  • Provide core app functionality, including saving, organizing, importing, exporting, and displaying recipes.
  • Power recipe scanning and cleanup features.
  • Improve app reliability and performance.
  • Respond to support requests.

RecipeSensei does not sell your personal information.

AI and Text Processing

RecipeSensei may use on-device or system-provided AI features to improve scanned recipe text quality, such as correcting OCR errors and formatting recipe details.

Data Storage and Retention

  • Recipe data is stored on your device and/or in Apple-managed services used by your device configuration, such as iCloud backups if enabled by you.
  • Exported recipe files are created only when you choose to share or export them.
  • Support communications are retained only as long as needed to respond and maintain service quality.

Sharing and Your Choices

RecipeSensei does not share your personal data with third parties for advertising. The app may rely on Apple platform services, such as app distribution and crash reporting, to operate.

  • You can edit or delete recipe data within the app.
  • You can choose whether to import, export, or share recipe files.
  • You can remove app access to photos or camera in iOS Settings.
  • You can uninstall the app to stop further app use.

Children's Privacy

RecipeSensei is not directed to children under 13, and Drake does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through the app.

Security and International Users

Reasonable safeguards are used for a mobile app context. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

If you use the app outside your country of residence, information may be processed where Apple or service infrastructure operates, subject to applicable law.
