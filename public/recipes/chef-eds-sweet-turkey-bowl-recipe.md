# Chef Ed's Sweet Turkey Bowl

My take on Chef Ed's submitted bowl idea featuring seasoned ground meat, roasted sweet potato, avocado, cottage cheese, Tajín, and lime.

## Submitted By

Chef Ed

Recipe credit goes to Edgar Sandoval.

## Original Submission

A bowl of:

- Ground turkey
- Sweet potato
- Avocado
- Cottage cheese
- Tajín

## Drake's Version

This version uses ground pork instead of ground turkey, plus onion, spices, roasted sweet potato, avocado, cottage cheese, Tajín, and lime.

## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground turkey or ground pork
- 1 small onion, diced
- 3 large sweet potatoes, microwaved briefly, cubed, and roasted
- 1 avocado, roughly chopped
- Cottage cheese, for topping
- Tajín, to taste
- 1 lime, for squeezing over the bowl
- Oil, for roasting the sweet potato
- Chili powder, to taste
- Garlic powder or garlic salt, to taste
- Cayenne pepper, to taste
- Black pepper, to taste

## Instructions

1. Microwave the sweet potato for a few minutes to soften it slightly.
2. Cube the sweet potato.
3. Toss the sweet potato cubes with oil, garlic salt, and pepper.
4. Roast at 450°F for about 20 minutes, or until browned and tender.
5. While the sweet potato roasts, dice the onion and saute it in a pan until softened.
6. Add the ground turkey or ground pork to the pan.
7. Season the meat with chili powder, garlic powder or garlic salt, cayenne pepper, and black pepper.
8. Cook until the meat is fully cooked through.
9. Build the bowl with the seasoned meat, roasted sweet potato, chopped avocado, and a dollop of cottage cheese.
10. Finish with a generous dash of Tajín and a squeeze of lime.
